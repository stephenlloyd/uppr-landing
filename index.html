Questions

Do I need to understand RIR or periodisation?

No. RIR is the science behind the progression, but you never need to think about it. Follow the workout, log your sets, answer two quick questions. The app handles everything else — like having a coach who never takes a day off.

How does this compare to other hypertrophy apps?

Apps like RP Hypertrophy and Juggernaut AI are excellent — they pioneered science-based training apps. We built on those foundations and added what we felt was missing: equipment awareness, AI plan import, coach plan conversion, multiple gym profiles for travel, discovery mode for new exercises, bonus workouts that don't break your plan, and exercise preferences that actually influence your sessions. The comparison table above is honest — see for yourself.

Can I import my coach's plan?

Yes — any format. Paste it into ChatGPT with our prompt builder and it converts text, spreadsheets, PDFs, even WhatsApp messages into something uppr can run. Your coach's programming is preserved — we layer your equipment profile on top and add automatic weekly progression.

What if I travel a lot?

Create equipment profiles for each situation — full gym, home gym, hotel room, bodyweight only. When you switch, the app intelligently swaps exercises that don't fit for alternatives that do. Same muscles, same stimulus, different equipment. Your plan doesn't skip a beat.

What if I want to do something different without messing up my plan?

Add a bonus workout — it doesn't affect your schedule at all. Or swap any exercise "just for today" — it reverts next session automatically. Or skip a day entirely — the queue just moves forward. Your plan works around your life.

Is my data private?

Yes. No ad trackers. No data sales. No third-party analytics. You can export everything as JSON/CSV or permanently delete your account and all data at any time.
