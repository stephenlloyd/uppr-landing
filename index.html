Questions

Do I need to understand RIR to use this?

No. RIR (Reps In Reserve) is the science behind the progression, but you don't need to think about it. Just follow the workout, log your sets, and answer two simple questions after each session. The app handles the rest.

What if I just want a quick workout?

Tap "Generate a Workout", pick your time and energy level, and you're training in seconds. No plan required. You can preview the exercises, swap any you don't like, and add your own.

Can I import a plan from my coach?

Yes. Paste your coach's programme into ChatGPT with our prompt builder, and it converts it into a format uppr can run. Works with text, spreadsheets, PDFs — anything. The coach's programming is preserved; uppr just adds automatic weekly progression on top.

Whatever you have. uppr works with barbells, dumbbells, cables, machines, bands, kettlebells, bodyweight, or any combination. Set up your equipment profile and every suggestion matches your setup.
