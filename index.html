Questions

What makes this different from Hevy or Strong?

Hevy and Strong are great loggers. uppr also tells you what to do next. It estimates your 1RM from every set, gives you weight and rep targets based on your actual strength, and adjusts your plan based on how you felt. Think logger vs. coach.

How does it compare to RP Hypertrophy or Juggernaut?

RP and Juggernaut pioneered science-based training apps. We added equipment awareness (they don't know what gym you're in), AI plan import, discovery mode, multiple gym profiles, and a free tier that's genuinely usable. The comparison table is honest.

Do I need to understand RIR or periodisation?

Nope. RIR is the engine under the hood. You just log your sets, rate how it felt, and the app handles progression. If you do understand it, the target RIR is shown on every exercise.

What's discovery mode?

First time doing an exercise? The app runs 2 quick calibration sets, one light, one heavier. From that it estimates your 1RM and calculates working weight. Takes about 4 minutes. After that, suggestions are based on your real strength.

Does it work offline?

Yes. The workout screen works with zero internet. Data syncs when you're back online. No dropped sets because you trained somewhere with no signal.

Can I import my coach's plan?

Any format. Use the built-in prompt builder with any AI to convert spreadsheets, PDFs, or text messages. Your coach's programming is preserved and the app layers equipment filtering and weekly progression on top.

What if I just want a quick workout?

Tap Quick Workout, pick your target muscles. Smart Coach checks what you've been neglecting and builds a session to fill the gaps. Uses exercises you've done before so there's no discovery overhead.

Is my data private?

Yes. No ad trackers. No data sales. No third-party analytics. Export everything or delete your account any time.
